About

Artistic Vision

Blending Italian heritage with contemporary California creativity, I approach each collaboration as a unique artistic expression. My work lives at the intersection of fashion, art, and storytelling.

I'm drawn to projects that value authenticity and artistic integrity. Whether it's a brand campaign, editorial shoot, or artistic project, I bring a European sensibility to the vibrant LA creative scene.

Based in Los Angeles and available throughout LA and Orange County, I seek partnerships with brands and creatives who share a passion for meaningful visual storytelling.
